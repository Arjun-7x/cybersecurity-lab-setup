# DVWA Attacks Menu 🍽️

Welcome to the DVWA **hacker feast**.  
Your Kali machine is hungry, and DVWA is a smorgasbord of vulnerabilities.  
Here’s what’s on the menu — all safe to practice inside your lab:

---

## 1. SQL Injection (SQLi) 🥩

- Hack into databases like a ghost in the shadows.  
- Learn how attackers exploit weak input fields to steal or manipulate data.  
- **Goal:** Extract user credentials, manipulate tables, or bypass login forms.  

> Think of it as sneaking into the kitchen and peeking at the secret recipes.

---

## 2. Cross-Site Scripting (XSS) 🍷

- Inject your own scripts into the web pages.  
- Watch the victim’s browser execute your commands.  
- **Goal:** Capture cookies, deface pages, or manipulate client-side behavior.  

> Like sprinkling a secret ingredient nobody notices… until it’s too late.

---

## 3. Command Injection 🔪

- Trick the server into executing your commands.  
- **Goal:** Take control of the server, list files, or get a reverse shell.  

> You’re not just peeking into the kitchen — you’re running the stove yourself.

---

## 4. File Inclusion (LFI / RFI) 📂

- Include local or remote files to expose secrets or gain control.  
- **Goal:** Access sensitive files, read `/etc/passwd`, or run malicious scripts.  

> Opening doors behind the kitchen walls. Don’t get caught.

---

## 5. CSRF (Cross-Site Request Forgery) 🕵️‍♂️

- Trick a logged-in user into performing actions without their knowledge.  
- **Goal:** Change passwords, make transactions, or manipulate accounts.  

> Like slipping a note into someone’s order without them realizing.

---

## 6. Brute Force / Weak Passwords 🔑

- Test weak passwords and login protections.  
- **Goal:** Crack accounts by trying common passwords.  

> Sometimes you don’t need finesse… just persistence and a hunger for access.

---

## 7. Security Levels: From Appetizer to Feast 🌶️

- **Low:** Safe for first bites, everything works.  
- **Medium:** Slightly tougher challenges, a real starter.  
- **High:** Full-on chef’s special, realistic vulnerabilities.  

> Start slow, savor the taste, then graduate to gourmet chaos.

---

## ⚠️ Lab Reminder

- Only attack DVWA inside your lab.  
- Never target real websites with these techniques.  

> The feast is yours… but the real world bites back.  
> Enjoy responsibly 😈.
